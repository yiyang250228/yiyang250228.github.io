Prediction of Hotel Price With Regression Models

Author: Yi Yang

Affiliation: School of Public Health, University of Michigan, USA

Date: April 2023

Venue: a Machine Learning and Biostatistics Online Course

Abstract

This technical report presents regression-based approaches for predicting hotel room prices using various machine learning techniques. The study compares model performances and discusses factors influencing hotel pricing. Experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of ensemble and deep-learning approaches in improving prediction accuracy.
